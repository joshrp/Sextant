# Contracts

Contracts are modelled as recipes on the **Contract** machine. Each contract recipe has an input (the product you pay) and an output (the product you receive). The solver treats contracts like any other recipe.

How many ships you need to meet that amount each month will depend on the size of ship & speed it's going, which is not calculated here. The unity cost is also not calculated here, but is the major cost of most contracts. 

## Contract Profitability

The **Contract Profitability** modifier in Zone Settings scales the output quantities of all contract recipes. Increase it to model higher trade profitability from edicts or research in your game.
