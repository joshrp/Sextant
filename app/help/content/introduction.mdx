# Introduction

Welcome to **Sextant** - a production planning tool for Captain of Industry

## Basics

- Drag and drop recipes to build your production chains
- The solver finds optimal production ratios to meet your [Goals](help?topic=goals)
- See exactly what inputs your factory needs to meet those goals and what by-products it produces
- Split Factories within a zone and create new zones to isolate groups of factories. 
- Import/Export to backup and share factories

## Features

- Research, Edicts and Difficulty bonuses are set per zone in [settings](settings/modifiers)
- [Balancers](help?topic=balancers) can be used to set up many-to-many connections that will choose the optimal production split for you
- [Recycling](help?topic=recycling) is split into Recycling and Scrap on each recipe to make routing scrap back into production chains easier.
- [Settlements](help?topic=settlements) to produce workers that can have their inputs and outputs toggled to reflect your tech level.
