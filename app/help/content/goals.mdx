# Goals

Goals define the production targets for your factory.

Goals tell the solver what you want to produce (outputs) or what resources you want to limit (inputs). The solver will optimize your factory to meet these goals.

## Setting Goals

Click the **+** button in the sidebar under goals and choose a type of goal

   - **Minimum of**: Produce at least this amount (≥)
   - **Exactly**: Produce exactly this amount (=)
   - **Maximum of**: Produce at most this amount (≤)

## Input Goals

Goals can also target input amounts as well as output amounts. Just enter a negative number and the factory will try to consume that amount (or at least / at most that amount depending on goal type).


## Managing Goals

- **Edit**: Click on a goal to modify its type or quantity
- **Remove**: Use the context menu to remove a goal
- **Add Producer**: Quickly add a recipe that produces this product

## Goal Status

Goals are color-coded in the sidebar:
- **Green**: Goal is fulfilled according to your constraint
- **Red**: Goal is not met or the factory cannot solve with current constraints

## Tips

- Start with output goals for what you want to produce
- Add input goals to limit resource usage
- Use "Exactly" goals to balance production precisely
- Use "Minimum" goals when you want surplus production
